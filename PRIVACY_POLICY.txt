Last Updated: February 9, 2026

MoniDex AI ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by MoniDex. This Privacy
Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when
you use the MoniDex AI mobile application and related services (collectively, the
"Service"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this
Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use
the App.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1. ACCOUNT INFORMATION. When you create an account using Google or Apple
     Sign-In, we collect the following information from your authentication
     provider:

     a) Full name;
     b) Email address;
     c) Profile image URL;
     d) Unique user identifier.

     We do not collect or store passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by
     Clerk, our third-party authentication provider.

1.2. CARD SCAN DATA. When you use the card scanning feature, we collect:

     a) Images of Pokemon TCG cards captured by your device camera;
     b) Card identification results, including card name, number, set, rarity,
        and type;
     c) Pricing and market data associated with identified cards;
     d) Scan history and metadata.

1.3. SUBSCRIPTION AND PURCHASE INFORMATION. When you subscribe to or purchase
     features within the App, the following information is collected and managed
     by RevenueCat and Apple:

     a) Subscription plan type (monthly, yearly, or lifetime);
     b) Purchase transaction records;
     c) Subscription status and renewal dates;
     d) App Store receipt data.

     We do not directly collect or store your credit card number, billing
     address, or other financial payment information. All payment processing is
     handled by Apple through the App Store.

1.4. DEVICE AND USAGE INFORMATION. We may automatically collect certain
     information about your device, including:

     a) Device type and operating system version;
     b) App version;
     c) General usage patterns within the App.

1.5. FEEDBACK. When you submit feedback through the in-app feedback feature in
     Settings, we collect the text of your message and associate it with your
     account. We use this information to respond to your inquiries, improve the
     App, and fix bugs.

1.6. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT. We want to be transparent about what we do
     not collect:

     a) We do not collect precise location data;
     b) We do not collect contacts or address book information;
     c) We do not collect microphone or audio data;
     d) We do not use advertising tracking or behavioral analytics SDKs;
     e) We do not collect health, financial, or biometric data.


2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1. We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

     a) To authenticate your identity and maintain your account;
     b) To process card images using AI-powered recognition and return card
        identification and pricing results;
     c) To display real-time and historical pricing data from third-party
        marketplaces;
     d) To manage your subscription and verify your access to premium features;
     e) To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries;
     f) To process feedback you submit through the App to improve our services
        and respond to you;
     g) To improve, maintain, and develop the App and its features;
     h) To enforce our Terms and Conditions and protect against misuse;
     i) To comply with legal obligations.


3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3.2. We share your information with the following third-party service providers
     who assist us in operating the App:

     a) CLERK (https://clerk.com) — Provides authentication and identity
        management. Clerk receives your name, email address, and profile image
        through the OAuth sign-in process. Clerk's privacy policy is available
        at https://clerk.com/legal/privacy.

     b) REVENUECAT (https://revenuecat.com) — Manages subscription and purchase
        data. RevenueCat receives your user identifier and App Store transaction
        data to manage your subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy is
        available at https://revenuecat.com/privacy.

     c) APPLE — Processes all in-app purchases and subscriptions through the App
        Store. Apple's privacy policy is available at
        https://apple.com/legal/privacy.

3.3. MONIDEX BACKEND. Card images and scan requests are transmitted to our
     backend servers at api.monidex.app for AI-powered card analysis. Our servers
     process card images to identify cards and retrieve pricing data from
     third-party sources including TCGPlayer and eBay.

3.4. We may also disclose your information if required by law, legal process,
     government request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect
     our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.


4. CARD IMAGE PROCESSING

4.1. When you scan a card, the App captures a photograph using your device
     camera. The image is cropped to the card area and compressed before being
     transmitted to our servers.

4.2. Card images are sent to our backend servers in base64-encoded format over
     a secure HTTPS connection for AI analysis.

4.3. Card images are processed in real time for the purpose of identifying the
     card and retrieving pricing information. We do not retain card images on our
     servers beyond the time necessary to complete the analysis and return
     results, unless required for service improvement or legal compliance.

4.4. You retain all ownership rights in the images you capture. By using the
     scanning feature, you grant us a limited license to process your images
     solely for the purpose of providing the Service, as described in our Terms
     and Conditions.


5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

5.1. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect
     your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration,
     disclosure, or destruction.

5.2. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted
     storage provided by the operating system.

5.3. All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using
     HTTPS/TLS protocols.

5.4. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of
     electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee
     absolute security of your data.


6. DATA RETENTION

6.1. We retain your account information for as long as your account remains
     active or as needed to provide you with the Service.

6.2. Card scan images are processed in real time and are not stored on our
     servers beyond the time necessary to complete the scan, unless required for
     service improvement or legal compliance.

6.3. Subscription and purchase records are retained by RevenueCat and Apple in
     accordance with their respective data retention policies.

6.4. Feedback you submit is retained for as long as your account is active or as

6.5. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete your personal data
     (including feedback you have submitted) from our active systems within
     thirty (30) days. Residual copies in backup systems will be purged within
